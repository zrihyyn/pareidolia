From March 2026...


Alex Rainer

Tom was born from a piece of leftover wood during a workshop. While working with scraps from the wood studio, I accidentally found a shape that looked like a dog’s face.



Stanisław Zieliński

Jamie came out of a conversation with Stan. He said something about pigeons, and around that time I was holding an A3 sheet and a cheap ballpoint pen. I just started drawing hundreds of pigeons on the paper. For a while they had no name. At some point I decided to call her Jamie. Stan also gave me a lot of thoughtful feedback on the website. He probably thinks I am a bit crazy for making all those drawings.



Haeun Na

She has been a great supporter of this drawing series and really encouraged me to keep going. She also makes amazing drawings herself.



Yin Engelaan

She once said she wants to get a tattoo of one of the drawings. I’m pretty sure she was joking… but still.



Bart de Baets

He encouraged me to develop Tom and Jamie further—maybe into comics—and shared references that sparked new ideas. At one point he even suggested designing wrapping paper for Page Not Found, an independent bookstore in The Hague.



Felix Salut
